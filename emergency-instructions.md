Emergency Instructions – Feluca Rooms
In case of emergency:

1. Gas smell
Leave the apartment immediately.

Do not use lights or electrical devices.

Inform us in the WhatsApp group.

Call the gas emergency service.

2. Fire or burning smell
Leave the apartment.

Call the fire department.

Inform us immediately.

3. Water leak or flooding
If possible, close the main water valve.

Send photos/videos in the WhatsApp group.

Wait for instructions.

4. Locked out / access problems
Send a message in the group.

We will check the smart access system or send assistance.
