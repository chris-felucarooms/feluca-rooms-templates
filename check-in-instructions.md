Check-in Instructions – Feluca Rooms
Welcome to Feluca Rooms!
Your check-in will follow the instructions below depending on your assigned method.

1. Smart Access Check-in
If your check-in is automated:

You will receive a personal access code in this WhatsApp group.

The code will be active from [date] at [time].

Enter the code on the smart panel at the building entrance.

If your room door is also smart, use the same code.

Send a confirmation message once inside the room.

Please share a quick photo of the room upon arrival.

2. In-person Check-in
If your check-in is in person:

We will meet at the apartment at the agreed time.

You will receive the keys and a short tour of the room and common areas.

3. Important Notes
Your contract must be signed before check-in.

Keep this WhatsApp group for all communication.

Report any issues immediately with photos or videos.
